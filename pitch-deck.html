02

The Problem

Real Estate Valuation in Dubai
is Broken

🏗️

No Transparent Pricing

Buyers rely on agent opinions and listing prices that can be 20-40% above or below actual market value. No independent, data-driven benchmark exists.

⏱️

Slow & Expensive

Traditional valuations take 3-7 days and cost AED 2,500-10,000. Investors making quick decisions miss opportunities or overpay.

📊

Fragmented Data

Transaction data, rental yields, service charges, and growth trends are scattered across DLD, RERA, and broker databases. No single source of truth.

$85 billion in Dubai real estate transactions in 2024 — yet most buyers make the biggest financial decision of their life based on guesswork.

03

The Solution

DubAIVal: Instant AI Valuations
in Under 5 Seconds

An Automated Valuation Model (AVM) purpose-built for Dubai real estate. Enter a building and unit — get a comprehensive valuation report with confidence scoring, investment signals, and market benchmarks.

How It Works

1. Enter Property Details

Building name, size, asking price, floor, view

2. Cascade AVM Engine Runs

Cross-references 6,800+ buildings, 287 area benchmarks, cluster analysis, view premiums, and live market data

3. Instant Valuation Report

Fair value estimate, PSF range, confidence score, yield analysis, investment signal, growth projections

4. AI-Powered Insights

Groq-powered AI analysis provides contextual market commentary and actionable recommendations

Key Differentiators

Multi-Layer Data Fusion

Building-level → Area-level → Cluster-level → Live market. 4 data layers for maximum accuracy.

Confidence Scoring

CoreLogic FSD-inspired confidence tiers (40-97%) so users know how reliable the estimate is.

Investment Intelligence

Price-to-Rent ratio, net yield, growth forecast, total return — not just a number, a full investment thesis.

Dubai-Native

Built specifically for Dubai's unique market: off-plan, freehold zones, DLD fees, RERA service charges.
