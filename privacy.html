Section 3

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and authenticate your account via Supabase Auth
  • To store and sync your portfolio data across your devices when you are signed in
  • To send price alert emails when a tracked building's average price moves past your threshold
  • To enable Deal Network listings and agent matching features
  • To send social media content on your behalf when you configure the Auto-Post engine (only with your explicit OAuth authorization)
  • To improve the AI valuation engine — we analyze aggregate, anonymized patterns in how the app is used, not individual property entries
  • To respond to support inquiries sent to our email address

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

Section 5

Data Storage & Security

Account data, deal listings, and price alerts are stored in Supabase (EU-Frankfurt region), protected by row-level security policies that ensure you can only access your own data. Supabase encrypts data at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.2+.

Portfolio data entered without signing in is stored only in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to our servers. If you sign in, your portfolio syncs to Supabase and is then subject to the same protections above.

OAuth tokens (Google, Meta) are encrypted by Supabase at rest and only accessed server-side by Vercel serverless functions — never exposed to the browser.

We implement reasonable security measures, but no system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users via the email address on file within 72 hours of becoming aware.

Section 7

Local Storage & Service Worker

DubAIVal uses your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Your portfolio assets (if not signed in)
  • App preferences (language, theme, recently visited sections)
  • Tour and onboarding state (so completed tours aren't shown again)
  • A session flag for Demo Mode

DubAIVal registers a service worker (sw.js) to enable offline use and faster repeat loads as a Progressive Web App. The service worker caches static app files only — not your personal data or API responses.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. No data stored in localStorage is shared with third parties.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time via your browser's "Clear site data" function, or by uninstalling the Android app.
