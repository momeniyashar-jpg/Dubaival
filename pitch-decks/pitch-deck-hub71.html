Industry Problem We Solve

The Fake Listing Crisis
& Why Our Deal Network is Different

The Problem with Current Platforms

✗
Fake Listings Everywhere

Agents on Property Finder & Bayut create fake listings with unrealistically low prices to attract buyers. Once a buyer calls, the agent redirects them to completely different properties. These misleading prices distort the market and confuse investors about real property values.

✗
Owner Privacy Violation

When an owner lists on Property Finder, hundreds of agents see it and immediately start calling the owner — not to buy, but to get the listing for themselves. Owners are bombarded with unsolicited calls from agents trying to sign exclusive agreements.

✗
Market Price Distortion

Fake listings with artificial prices create a false picture of the market. Buyers think properties are available at prices that don't exist. Sellers can't determine fair value. The entire market suffers from misinformation.

✗
Buyers Overpay Without Knowing

Due to lack of independent valuation data, many transactions close at prices 15-30% above actual market value. Buyers only discover they overpaid when they try to resell or refinance — by then it's too late.

DubAIVal Deal Network Solution

★
Title Deed Verification — Zero Fakes

Every "I Have" listing requires a Title Deed number — the official DLD document proving ownership. Agents can also upload Title Deed photos. It is physically impossible to create a fake listing. No Title Deed = no listing. Period.

★
Privacy-First — Owner Protection

Owner contact details and property photos are hidden by default. Interested buyers must send an inquiry. The owner sees each request and manually approves or rejects every buyer. No more agent spam. No unsolicited calls. The owner controls who sees their property.

★
Real Prices Only — Market Integrity

Because every listing is tied to a verified Title Deed with real owner information, prices reflect actual market value. Combined with our AVM engine, buyers can instantly compare the listed price against our independent valuation — eliminating deception entirely.

★
AVM + Deal Network = Trust Engine

Every listed property gets an automatic DubAIVal verdict: is this price fair, undervalued, or overpriced? Buyers don't rely on agent claims — they have independent AI-powered valuation right next to every listing.

In Summary: Property Finder is an open marketplace where anyone can post anything at any price. DubAIVal is a verified marketplace where every listing is backed by a Title Deed, every price is validated by AI, and every owner's privacy is protected. This is how real estate platforms should work.
