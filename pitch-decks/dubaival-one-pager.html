The Problem

Dubai's AED 761 billion real estate market has no independent property valuation tool. 110,000 new investors entered in 2024, relying on broker opinions and asking prices — not data. Traditional valuations cost AED 2,500-15,000 and take 3-7 days. Listing platforms are flooded with fake listings and manipulated prices.

Our Solution

DubAIVal provides instant, AI-powered property valuations — free, accurate, and independent. Our Cascade AVM engine uses 4-layer data fusion across 10,880 DLD-verified properties and 347 area benchmarks to deliver building-level price estimates, confidence scores, investment signals, and rental analysis in under 5 seconds.
